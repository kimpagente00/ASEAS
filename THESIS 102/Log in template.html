ASEAS
A Student Enrolment Advising System

A decision support system (DSS) is a computerized program that aids in the making of
decisions, judgements, and actions in a company or organization. This study aims to develop
a Decision Support System (DSS) for online enrollment and reduce the time and improve the
enrollment system by adding features that would greatly benefit the St. Peter's College of Iligan.
